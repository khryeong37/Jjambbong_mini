ATOM vs ATOMONE — Price (top) + Activity (below)

Row1: Price (actual, USD) Row2: ATOMONE — Activity = Tx (lower) + Volume (upper) Row3: ATOM — Activity = Tx (lower) + Volume (upper)

How It’s Calculated

Tx_norm = minmax (sum(Tx, 7d))
Volume_norm = minmax (sum(Volume, 7d))
ActivitySum = Tx_norm + Volume_norm (0–2)
